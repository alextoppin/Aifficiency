Founder, Aifficiency

Alex is an ex-management consultant with experience spanning SMEs through to FTSE 250 and 500 companies. He founded Aifficiency to bring structured, enterprise-grade thinking to smaller businesses that need it most — without the cost or complexity of a large firm.

The mission is simple: help SMEs understand where AI can genuinely help, make a confident decision about what to do, and implement it properly.
