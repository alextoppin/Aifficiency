// Background

Alex is an ex-management consultant with experience spanning SMEs through to FTSE 250 and 500 companies. He founded Aifficiency to bring structured, enterprise-grade thinking to smaller businesses — without the cost or complexity of a large firm.

His consulting career gave him hands-on experience across the full engagement lifecycle — from initial discovery through to live implementation. That means he doesn't just produce recommendations; he knows what it takes to make them stick.
