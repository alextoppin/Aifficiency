Each tier stands alone — you're never locked in. Run Discover and take the findings in-house. Commission Evaluate and hand the business case to your own team. Or go end-to-end.

// Common questions

Do I have to buy all three tiers? +
No. Each tier is available independently. We'll always recommend the approach that makes most sense for your situation.
How are fixed prices set? +
We scope each engagement on a free initial call and provide a fixed price before work begins. The figures shown are starting points for typical SME engagements.
We're not very technical — is that a problem? +
Not at all. Aifficiency is built for SMEs that are not AI-native. We translate complex technology into plain business language.
How does time and materials work? +
We agree an estimated day range and day rate upfront. You receive weekly timesheets and can pause or extend as needed.
