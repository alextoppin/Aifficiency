Self-assessment

Is AI right
for my business?

Not every business is ready for AI — and not every problem needs it. Work through the checklist below to get an honest sense of whether AI is likely to add value in your business.

// Tick everything that applies to your business

We have tasks that repeat on a daily or weekly basis
Data entry, reporting, scheduling, invoicing — anything that happens regularly and follows a pattern.
Manual errors cost us time or money to fix
Mistakes in orders, invoices, or data that have to be caught and corrected.
We struggle to get useful insight from our data
Data sits in systems that are hard to interrogate. Decisions are made on gut feel more than they should be.
Our team spends time on low-value admin
Capable people doing work that doesn't really need them.
We handle a high volume of similar customer enquiries
Status updates, FAQs, appointment requests — things that follow predictable patterns.
Growth means hiring — the model doesn't improve with scale
More customers means more staff to service them.
We produce regular documents or reports that follow a template
Weekly reports, client updates, proposals — produced repeatedly from the same structure.
Data moves between systems manually
Copy-pasting between spreadsheets or platforms. Information that doesn't flow automatically.
Leadership has appetite to invest in improving operations
AI projects need buy-in to succeed.
We have a specific, felt pain point — not just a vague desire to be more efficient
The best AI projects solve specific, visible problems.

// Strong signals AI will help

Your business is well-placed if...

  • ✓You have high-volume, repetitive processes that follow consistent rules
  • ✓You can clearly describe what "good" looks like for the process
  • ✓You have data — even messy data — that records what's happening
  • ✓Leadership is willing to invest time as well as money
  • ✓You have a specific, felt pain point — not just a general desire to improve

// Signs it might not be the right time

Worth pausing if...

  • ✕Your core processes aren't documented and no one fully understands them
  • ✕Leadership isn't aligned on the need to change
  • ✕You're mid-restructure, merger, or major systems change
  • ✕You want AI as a silver bullet for a deeper commercial problem
  • ✕The budget simply isn't there — underfunded AI projects rarely deliver

// Honesty matters

AI isn't the answer
to everything.

We'd rather tell you early that AI isn't the right fit than take you through a full engagement delivering something that doesn't make a real difference.

// When AI isn't the answer

  • The process is too bespoke or judgment-heavy to automate reliably
  • The problem is actually a people or management issue, not a process one
  • A simpler fix — a better system, a clearer SOP — would solve it faster and cheaper
  • The volume or frequency of the task doesn't justify the investment
  • The data needed simply doesn't exist yet
